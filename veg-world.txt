-Veg World-

Concept:
You are a carrot at first and you should explore the vegetables
world by interacting with other vegetables. You can choose more than
one character when you finish levels.The objective is to eliminate every 
single member of the snack clan in order to save humanity in their eating
habitudes.

Technology to use:
HTML canvas with JavaScript

Skills to have:
-Tilemap creation in canvas
-Character design with paint
-Tilesets in paint
